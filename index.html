Resume

Elisabeth Kaufhold first studied recorder with Birgit Beyer, Prof. Peter Thalheimer and Prof. Jeremias Schwarzer at the Musikhochschule in Nuremberg from 1998 to 2002. There she also began studying the baroque bassoon with Ursula Bruckdorfer, which she deepened in postgraduate studies at the Schola Cantorum in Basel with Donna Agrell and in Frankfurt with Christian Beuse. At the same time, she began postgraduate studies in recorder at the Musikhochschule in Zurich with Matthias Weilenmann. Already during her studies she performed in renowned baroque orchestras such as the Akademie für Alte Musik Berlin, the Collegium 1704 Prague under Vaclav Luks or the Capella Augustina under Andreas Spering. She can be heard in numerous radio and CD recordings. Since 2007, the freelance artist lives and works in Berlin.
